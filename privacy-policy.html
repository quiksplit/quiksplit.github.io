QuikSplit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all QuikSplit services, including our website and mobile applications. By using QuikSplit, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Quick Overview:

  • QuikSplit is a private ledger for tracking expenses with friends. Expenses are only visible to those you share them with.
  • We collect basic registration information (name, email, phone number, profile picture) and expense data to provide the service.
  • We use Google AdMob for ads and Stripe for payments but do not share your personal information for marketing purposes without consent.
  • You control your data and can manage privacy settings in your account.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all QuikSplit services, including our website and mobile applications. By using QuikSplit, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Registration Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and an optional profile picture. These are required to create an account, allow friends to find you, and enable secure access from any device.
  • User-Generated Content: We store expense descriptions, amounts, payments, comments, and receipt images you upload.
  • Invitations: When you invite others to join QuikSplit, we collect their contact information (name, email, or phone number) as provided by you.
  • Device and Location Information: We collect device type, IP address, and location data for security and to personalize ads via Google AdMob.
  • Payment Information: For the paid receipt-scanning feature, Stripe collects your payment details (e.g., credit/debit card information). We do not store this data directly.
  • Third-Party Integrations: If you connect external services (e.g., Stripe), we may collect related data, such as payment status.

3. How We Use Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide, support, and secure the QuikSplit service, including recording expenses and calculating balances.
  • Communicate with you about your account (e.g., phone verification codes via SMS).
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • Personalize ads via Google AdMob based on your usage and location.

We will not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information as follows:

  • With Friends and Group Members: Your name, email, phone number, and profile picture are visible to those you share expenses with, based on your settings.
  • With Service Providers: We use third parties (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) to support our operations. They are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for QuikSplit purposes.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose information to comply with laws, respond to legal processes (e.g., subpoenas), or protect safety and security.
  • Business Transfer: If QuikSplit is sold or merged, your information may be transferred to the new entity, which will assume these privacy obligations.

5. Security

We use encryption and other technical measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your account and using safe practices (e.g., strong passwords). We will notify you via email or our website of any significant data breach affecting your unencrypted information, as required by law.

6. Cookies and Tracking

  • We use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and ad personalization.
  • Google AdMob may use tracking tools to deliver targeted ads based on your usage and location. These third parties may collect data over time and across sites, but we do not provide them with personally identifiable information.
  • You can manage cookie preferences via your browser or opt out of personalized ads through Google's settings (visit Google's Ad Settings).

7. User Choices

  • Access and Update: Review and edit your information in your account settings.
  • Communications: We only send SMS for phone verification. You can remove your phone number to stop this, though it may affect account security.
  • Location Tracking: Disable location services on your device to limit collection.
  • Delete Account: Close your account via settings. Data shared with others (e.g., expenses) may remain visible to them.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. Upon account deletion, we will remove your data within 90 days, though some may persist in backups for legal reasons.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are effective upon posting. The latest version will be available at quiksplit.luistodobom.com/privacy.

10. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact us at quiksplit.app@gmail.com.
